## n8n

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform that lets developers and teams build powerful automations combining the speed of no-code with the flexibility of full code. With over 400,000 GitHub stars and used by thousands of companies worldwide, n8n is one of the fastest-growing tools in the developer automation space.

As a Full Stack Engineer on the Nodes team at n8n, I work on the integrations that power n8n's automation capabilities — building and maintaining nodes that connect developers' workflows to the hundreds of apps and services they rely on.

**Stack:** TypeScript, Vue, NodeJS
